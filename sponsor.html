How to sponsor it?

  • Non-negotiable: only one sponsor and one text link.

    You will get one colored text link (adblock resistant - no JS) on the top of the all pages and on the quarterly newsletter. 140 Unicode characters allowed. As a call for attention - quarterly - you may change the link color.

  • Last (and minimum) price of the main page: $49 quarterly.

    If the spot is taken, you may propose a price «raise» for next quarter. Two weeks before the end of the quarter, the incubent will be informed of the highest raise - if applicable - updating next quarter’s sponsoring price. The incumbent may «call» the price and keep the spot. The actual price will be updated here and visible to all.

  • Past traffic (not using adblock!) is showed here.

    Most traffic is direct. Most people found Slower News on Hacker News.

  • Paypal or crypto.

    Don’t expect receipts. Like Slower News itself, the process is analogic and quixotic. Hopefully trustable.
  • Even if you found something less palatable say hi, maybe we can work it out.

What is Slower News?

These are, somehow, curated news extracting relevant trends and some edge cases for borderline nerds, that don’t want to miss out, nor spend a shit-ton of time filtering trivia.

Why?

I wanted to read something like this - an information flood is drowning our times - but I couldn’t find it elsewhere. So, as most projects, it started while «scratching my own itch» and before becoming a website it lived as a markdown file.

Making sense of chaos is a sisyphean task but I see it as a way of using my procrastination positively, I’m an analist by heart.

How do you curate articles?

I cherry-pick articles that dissect trends or unveil lesser known trends and interesting edge cases, and are relevant, at least, for some months. It’s certainly affected by my biases (I’m aware of these) and most articles were on Hacker News. The articles’ listing order is not random.

How often do you update Slower News?

Only when relevant. New articles are easy to spot but there is RSS and a newsletter also.

Can I contribute?

Open an issue or pull request the repository with relevant links, corrections or suggestions. I’m curating only a few pages but I accept pull requests creating/curating more - if you can handle the burden of using raw HTML. For now, this is my filter to accept them.

Colophon

Hand coded - fully functional without JS - and statically hosted at Netlify via a Github repository. Tufte CSS (adapted) with native darkmode. No images, no tracking (GDPR friendly analytics with Goatcounter) and no bloat. Ok, it uses non-standard fonts but web design is mostly typography.
